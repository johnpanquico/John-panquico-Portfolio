KHRONOS Civil Registry System

KHRONOS is an online Local Civil Registry web portal designed for applying for civil registry certificates, including birth certificates, death certificates, marriage certificates, and other certificate-related requests.

The system has two main sides: the client side and the admin side. The client side allows applicants to submit certificate applications online, encode required information, upload supporting documents, and proceed with the application process. The admin side is intended for authorized civil registry personnel who review, validate, approve, and process the submitted certificate applications.

Game Development

Equatia

Equatia is one of my freelance client projects developed to support a research study. It is a mathematics-based role-playing game (RPG) designed to combine interactive gameplay with educational content.

The game allows players to explore the environment, interact with non-player characters, complete quests, and answer mathematics-related questions as part of the core gameplay experience. It was designed to make learning more engaging through adventure-style progression and challenge-based activities.

For this project, I used Unity and C# for development, Blender for selected design assets, and JSON as a lightweight data-saving structure.
